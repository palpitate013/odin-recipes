Pot Pie

Description

Chicken pot pie, a classic comfort food dish that's perfect for a cozy night in! Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make this deliciousness:

Ingredients

Filling:
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or half-and-half
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Crust:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1/4 cup ice-cold water

Steps

Step 1: Make the Filling
  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet or sauté pan, melt 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of butter.
  6. Once melted, add the diced onion, minced garlic, sliced carrots, and celery stalks.
  7. Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 8-10 minutes.
  8. Add the flour to the skillet and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  9. Gradually stir in the chicken broth and heavy cream or half-and-half.
  10. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for 2-3 minutes, until thickened slightly.
  11. Stir in the cooked chicken and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Step 2: Make the Crust
  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  2. Add the cold butter pieces and use a pastry blender or your fingers to work them into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the ice-cold water, stirring with a fork until the dough comes together in a ball.
Step 3: Assemble the Pie
  1. On a lightly floured surface, roll out half of the crust dough to a thickness of about 1/8 inch (3 mm).
  2. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Pour the chicken filling over the dough in the baking dish.
  4. Roll out the remaining crust dough to the same size as before and place it on top of the filling.
  5. Crimp the edges of the pie crust to seal the filling inside.
Step 4: Bake the Pie
  1. Brush the top of the pie with a little bit of milk or beaten egg for a golden glaze.
  2. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, until the crust is golden brown and the filling is hot and bubbly.
Step 5: Serve
  1. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.
  2. Enjoy your delicious homemade chicken pot pie!
