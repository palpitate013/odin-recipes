Hamburgers

Description

Hamburgers, a classic American favorite, perfect for BBQs and casual dinners! Hereâ€™s how you can make your own delicious hamburgers from scratch:

Ingredients

Burgers:
  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
Toppings (optional):
  • Leafy lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

Step 1: Prepare the Beef
  1. In a large bowl, gently mix the ground beef with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty about 1/2 inch thick.
Step 2: Cook the Burgers
  1. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Place the patties on the grill or skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or longer if you prefer your burgers more well-done.
  3. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking and cover to melt.
Step 3: Toast the Buns
  1. While the burgers are cooking, slice the buns in half and toast them on the grill or in a toaster until golden brown.
Step 4: Assemble the Burgers
  1. Place each cooked burger patty on the bottom half of a toasted bun.
  2. Add your desired toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles.
  3. Spread ketchup and mustard on the top half of the bun, then place it on top of the burger.
Step 5: Serve
  1. Serve your homemade hamburgers with your favorite side dishes and enjoy!
